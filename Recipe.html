    • Beans Cake

    Ingredient Quantity
    Beans 4 Cups
    Palm Oil 200ml
    Spices 20grams
    Seasonngs 5grams
    Salt 1 Spoons

    Instruction

    1. Soak the beans in water for about 30 minutes to soften the skins.
    2. After soaking, remove the skins by rubbing the beans together in water and rinsing repeatedly until they are clean and skinless.
    3. Place the cleaned beans in a blender, adding a small amount of water to create a smooth, thick paste.
    4. Add spices (such as pepper and onions) to the blender for additional flavor. Blend until smooth.
    5. Transfer the blended beans paste to a bowl and add salt and seasonings. Mix well to ensure even distribution.
    6. Heat palm oil in a deep frying pan or pot over medium heat until hot.
    7. Using a spoon, scoop small portions of the bean mixture and carefully drop them into the hot oil.
    8. Fry each side for about 3-5 minutes or until the bean cakes are golden brown.
    9. Remove the bean cakes from the oil using a slotted spoon and drain them on paper towels to remove excess oil.
    10. Serve hot as a snack or breakfast item, often enjoyed with a side of pap (corn porridge) or a spicy pepper sauce.

    Tea

    Ingredient Quantity
    Water 500ml
    Sugar 2 Spoons
    Milk 5 Spoons
    Bournvita 3 Spoons
    Tea Bag 1 Pcs

    Instruction

    1. Pour 500 ml of water into a kettle or pot and bring it to a boil.
    2. Once the water boils, place the tea bag in a cup and pour the hot water over it.
    3. Let the tea bag steep in the hot water for about 3-5 minutes, depending on how strong you want the tea flavor. Remove the tea bag after steeping.
    4. Stir in the sugar and milk, adjusting the amounts if you prefer a different level of sweetness or creaminess.
    5. Add 3 tablespoons of Bournvita to the tea and stir thoroughly until it dissolves completely, giving the tea a smooth, chocolatey taste.
    6. Your tea with milk, sugar, and Bournvita is ready! Serve hot, and enjoy this comforting, flavorful drink.

    Cake

    Ingredient Quantity
    Water 1000ml
    Sugar 5 Spoons
    Milk 5 Spoons
    Flour 4 Cups
    Butter 1 Packet

    Instruction

    1. Preheat your oven and grease a cake pan with a bit of butter or line it with parchment paper.
    2. In a large mixing bowl, cream the butter and sugar together until the mixture is light and fluffy. This helps to give the cake a good texture.
    3. Slowly add the milk and continue to mix until itâ€™s well combined with the butter and sugar.
    4. Gradually add the flour to the wet ingredients, mixing gently with a spoon or spatula. Be careful not to overmix, as this can make the cake dense.
    5. Slowly add small amounts of water as needed to achieve a smooth, thick, but pourable batter. You may not need the entire 1000 ml of water; add just enough to get the right consistency.
    6. Pour the butter into the prepared cake pan and spread it evenly.
    7. Place the pan in the preheated oven and bake for 35-45 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
    8. Once baked, allow the cake to cool in the pan for 10-15 minutes, then transfer it to a wire rack to cool completely.
    9. Slice and serve your homemade cake!
